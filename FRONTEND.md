# Frontend User Guide

## Getting Started

### First Time Setup

1. **Sign Up**: Create a new account with your email and password
2. **Complete Profile**: Add your name and bio
3. **Start Following**: Follow other users to see their posts in your feed
4. **Create Posts**: Share your thoughts with the world!

## Features

### Navigation
- **Home**: View your personalized feed
- **Profile**: View your profile and posts
- **Logout**: Sign out of your account

### Creating Posts
1. Click the text area that says "What's on your mind?"
2. Type your post content (up to 1000 characters)
3. Optionally add images
4. Click "Post" to publish

### Interacting with Posts
- **Like**: Click the heart icon to like a post
- **Comment**: Click the comment icon to view and add comments
- **Delete**: If it's your post, click the Ã— button to delete

### User Profiles
- Click on a username to view their profile
- See their bio and profile picture
- View all their posts
- Follow or unfollow them

### Comments
- View all comments on a post by clicking the comment icon
- Add your own comment in the text area
- Delete your own comments

## Tips & Tricks

1. **Character Limit**: Posts have a 1000 character limit
2. **Follow Users**: Follow users whose content interests you
3. **Edit Your Profile**: Update your bio and profile picture anytime
4. **Search**: You can copy-paste usernames from URLs to visit profiles

## Troubleshooting

### Can't login?
- Check your email and password are correct
- Make sure you've registered first

### Posts not showing?
- Try refreshing the page
- Make sure you're following users or you have created posts

### Images not uploading?
- Check file size (keep under 5MB)
- Ensure it's a valid image format (JPG, PNG, GIF)

## Best Practices

1. **Be Respectful**: Treat other users with respect
2. **Original Content**: Share your own thoughts and experiences
3. **No Spam**: Don't spam or flood the feed
4. **Privacy**: Don't share sensitive personal information
5. **Report Issues**: Report inappropriate content

## Keyboard Shortcuts

- **Tab**: Navigate between elements
- **Enter**: Submit forms (when focused on a button)
- **Escape**: Close modals or dialogs

## Privacy & Security

- Your password is encrypted and secure
- Logout when using public computers
- Don't share your authentication token
- Report suspicious activity to support

## Accessibility

- The app is fully keyboard navigable
- Color contrast meets WCAG standards
- Screen reader friendly
- Touch-friendly on mobile devices
